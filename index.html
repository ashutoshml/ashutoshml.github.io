About Me


Hello World!,

Interest

I love to code, sleep, play football and table tennis (not necessarily in that order).

I have had good amount of exposition in Algorithms, Logic (Propositional and predicate) and have also dabbled with Combinatorics, Set theory, Ramsey theory, Graph Theory, Programming Language Concepts, Program Analysis and topics alike.
